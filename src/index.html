About HackUPC

HackUPC is the first student hackathon in Spain. This will be our sixth edition with 700 awesome hackers and lots of side activities to learn new technologies, meet interesting people and have fun. Come make history with us!

The check-in is from 4 p.m. to 6:45 p.m. on Friday, October 11. The opening ceremony starts at 7:00 p.m., also on Friday. We expect to finish HackUPC by 5:00 p.m. on Sunday 13th. HackUPC will take place in UPC's North Campus FIB buildings (A3, A4, A5 and A6) in sunny Barcelona.

HackUPC is free for all accepted hackers. We will provide you with WiFi, swag, spaces enabled to work and meals including breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks during the night. Showers are opened every morning for grooming and mattresses to relax in sleep-enabled classrooms are also provided.

Webpages, mobile apps, hardware projects, games, an API... Anything is on the table. We have a variety of hardware components, from Arduino kits and Raspberry Pi's, to Amazon Echo dots and Myo Arm. You can borrow any Hardware Lab component or bring your own!

All attendees must bring a valid government ID for admission. Other common checklist items include laptops, chargers, adapters, sleeping bags and toiletries. We encourage hackers to shower, so bring a towel!

Projects will be judged based on technical difficulty, innovation and overall learning experience mainly, but also on their usability and design, by a panel of industry judges and UPC faculty.

Since beta 1.0, we support vegan, vegetarian, no pork, gluten-free and other restrictions like halal.

Travel reimburshment

Of course! The quantity varies depending on where you come from: we are giving up to 50€ for Spaniards, and up to 100€ if you come from abroad. We want to give all of you the opportunity of joining us! However, you must remember that not all participants will be eligible to receive travel reimbursement.

During your application process, there is a field for TR, fill it if you want to request it. In case it doesn't appear that means that we have run out of TR. This option cannot be changed once you've been accepted so pay attention while you're doing your application!

To receive TR you need to upload your project to HackUPC devpost and present it on demos. Please note that teams of more than 4 members as well as those breaking any other HackUPC rule may not have TR guaranteed.

Applications

Students from all around the world which are at least 14 years old can apply. University, vocational training, high school, etc., students are all welcome! For under 18 cases, we will request a letter signed by parents or legal tutor to attend.

Try to make a difference amongst other hackers, be creative! However, above all, remember to be humble. In our Medium page, you can find a great post in which are detailed all the steps to make your application even greater. Make sure you check it out!

As long as you graduated no longer than a year ago, you can still be accepted as a hacker. Nevertheless, if that isn’t your case and you don’t want to miss HackUPC, you can apply as a mentor!

Teams

It's completely fine! In fact, you come here to learn, so you don't need any previous experience. We would love to receive your application!

No need to worry! Part of the fun of a hackathon is meeting new people. We will have a team building activity at the beginning of the event for everyone to meet and form teams.

Teams of <= 4 members are welcome. Have in mind that if your team is bigger, you won’t be able to participate in any HackUPC competition or challenge.
